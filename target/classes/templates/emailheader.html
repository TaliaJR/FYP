The email header is one of the two main parts of an email, with the other being the email body. The header is a snippet of HTML (the language that makes up emails) code that contains important data about the email such as: the sender's email address, the recipient's email address, the time and date the email was sent and received, the sender's SMTP server (please see the section 'server investigation' for more information on this) and the sender's IP address.
