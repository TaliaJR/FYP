Email phishing is a online based scam that uses e-mail communication in order to gain something, usually this something is financial gain. Scammers achieve this by sending e-mails to people pretending to be someone from a legitimate company, such as a bank or the Government. These e-mails usually create an atmosphere of urgency, which causes the user to act rash. Financial gain is either achieved by the recipient directly sending the phisher money or by the phisher gaining credentials that allow them to access the victim's funds, which can be achieved by asking the victim to sign in on a fake website.
